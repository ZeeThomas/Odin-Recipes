BlueBerry Muffins

Perfectly delicious vegan blueberry muffins that the whole family will enjoy. Perfect for a random delicious treat or for your kids bake sale. Giving everything you love without the things you don't

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cup unsweetened soy milk
  • 2 cups of all purpose flour
  • 2tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • Avocado oil
  • 1 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/3 cup canola oil(vegetable oil)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh blueberries

Steps:

  1. Combine the soy milk(1 1/4 cup) and apple cider vinegar (1 tsp)in a measuring cup and set aside to curdle. Ths is your buttermilk.
  2. Preheat oven to 400Â° spray pan lightly with oil and line pan with liners.
  3. In a mixing bowl, whisk together all the dry ingredients: Flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and cornstarch
  4. Pour in the soy milk/vinegar mixture, canola oil, and vanilla. Stir w/ large spoon to combine. Don't overmix. Some small lumps are fine.
  5. Fold in blueberries gently. Using 1/4 cup measuring cup, add batter to the liners in the pan almost all the way full. Top w/ extra blueberries.
  6. Bake for 22-26 min (I did 26) until golden brown on top and the toothpick is clean. Let cool and eventually store in airtight container.
